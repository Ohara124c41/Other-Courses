Bank as a platform.

Transparency as an asset.

The Open Bank Project is an open source API and App store for banks that empowers financial institutions to securely and rapidly enhance their digital offerings using an ecosystem of 3rd party applications and services.

For Banks

Bring innovation to your door and quickly deliver a greater range of apps and services.
Learn more

For Developers

Build the next generation of banking apps and deploy to a wide range of banks.

For Customers

Enjoy a wide range of banking applications that address your needs and appeals to you!
